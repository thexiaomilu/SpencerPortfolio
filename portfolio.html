Perspective Design

Perspective Design

This happened in 2100. After decades of human destruction of the earth, there is very little space for human living on the earth.

People established a new government called "World Government", which mainly controls the reproduction of human beings and plans the future of human beings. The government issued a new form of currency called "Life Points", which can be used for your life. Our story mostly revolves around this.


Poster Design



Demo Video

This video illustrates what happened to two people.

The first one is our male protagonist, Jun. Jun is an ordinary person, he has basically spent his life in a dormant cabin from birth to now, and his dormant cabin is near Jupiter, which is far away from the earth. His job is to control the robot to help him grow and collect algae to earn "Life Points", which can maintain his life.

The second is our heroine Rachael. She is a "taste tester" born in a rich family. She makes money by eating food, collecting the taste of food through instruments, and selling these foods. This job is like a current Internet celebrity, Jun is one of Rachael's fans.
