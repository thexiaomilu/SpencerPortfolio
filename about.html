All About

Hello, My name is Siming Qiao. As for my character, I think I am qualified for all kinds of design. Possessing vital and broad interests in art and design, I commenced studying sketching during my childhood. Up till now, I have been used to plunging myself into the world of art, in which not only could I keep being far from distractions, but my geometrical thinking is cultivated and enhanced. With equal passion, I enjoy immersing myself in conducting research and designing.

Benefiting from my undergraduate study in Graphic Design, I have learned relevant knowledge and developed strong logical analysis ability and innovation ability. Therefore, I am good at analyzing effective methods more rationally in projects and selecting the most efficient means through scientific investigation and research.
